🏆 THE LEAGUE

“Not just a game. A revolution in competition.”

🔥 What is the Overtime Elite League?

The OTE League is the world’s first fully-augmented competitive basketball league, powered by the VSPX system. It merges real athleticism with immersive tech for a next-gen sports experience.

12 pro teams battle it out on dynamic, AI-responsive courts. Players range from global draft picks to homegrown OTE prodigies.

📊 League Format

  • Season Length: 18 weeks
  • Games Per Team: 2 per week (1 home, 1 away)
  • Home Advantage: Teams play in custom AR arenas (e.g., Cyber Jungle, Lunar Arena)
  • Playoffs: Top 8 teams enter a single-elimination bracket
  • Finals: Best-of-3 at the annual Overtime Summit

⚙️ OTE Mode – Rules of the Game

  • Shot Clock: 18 seconds
  • Dynamic Court Zones: AR zones adapt mid-play via AI
  • Momentum Boosts: "Heat Zones" unlock after 3-score streaks
  • Holo-Referees: AI officiating with instant infraction calls
  • Power Player Mode: Once-per-game focus boost for a player

🧠 Player Stats & Progression

Every player wears a NeuroChip S2, tracking:

  • Reaction Time
  • Decision Speed
  • Haptic Balance
  • Court Awareness
  • Jump & Sprint Efficiency

Their Dynamic Performance Index (DPI) updates after every game, influencing rankings, bonuses, and draft stock.

🛡️ Team Identities (Examples)
